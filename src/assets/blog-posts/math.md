# Can you write Math?

> Answer: Yes you can!

Watch this:

$$
\begin{aligned}
x^2 + 2x &= 3 \\
(x + 1)^2 - 2 &= 3 \\
x &= \sqrt{5} - 1 \quad \text{or} \quad x = -\sqrt{5} - 1
\end{aligned}
$$
